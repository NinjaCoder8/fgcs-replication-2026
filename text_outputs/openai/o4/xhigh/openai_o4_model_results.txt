Below is one possible way to organize the 16 “actors” into four high-level dimensions that reflect the major concerns and responsibilities in an end-to-end MLOps lifecycle. For each dimension we list the relevant actors and propose one leading actor who typically owns or drives that dimension in most organizations.

Dimension               | List of Actors                                                                 | Proposed Leading Actor  
----------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------ | -----------------------  
1. Business & Strategy  | • Business Stakeholder  
                         • Manager  
                         • Domain Expert  
                         • Solution Architect                                               | Business Stakeholder  
2. Data & Feature        | • Data Engineer  
                         • Data Steward  
                         • Data Scientist  
                         • Scientist  
                         • Data Analyst (implied)                                           | Data Engineer  
3. Model Development     | • ML Engineer / Developer  
                         • Data Scientist  
                         • Software Developer (for custom code)  
                         • Application Developer                                           | ML Engineer / Developer  
4. Infrastructure & Ops  | • DevOps Engineer  
                         • MLOps Engineer  
                         • Operations Engineer  
                         • IT Operations / Professional                                    | MLOps Engineer  

Explanation of dimensions and leads:

1. Business & Strategy  
   – Focus: Defining the business problem, requirements, ROI, success criteria and high-level solution architecture.  
   – Lead: Business Stakeholder drives goals, budget and prioritization.  

2. Data & Feature  
   – Focus: Data ingestion, cleansing, transformation, cataloging, feature engineering and data governance.  
   – Lead: Data Engineer owns pipelines, schemas, versioning and quality.  

3. Model Development  
   – Focus: Algorithm selection, coding/training models, experimentation, hyperparameter tuning and packaging.  
   – Lead: ML Engineer / Developer coordinates model building, integrates science and software.  

4. Infrastructure & Ops  
   – Focus: Resource provisioning, CI/CD pipelines, containerization, deployment, scaling, monitoring and maintenance.  
   – Lead: MLOps Engineer ensures repeatable, automated, secure operations of data and models.
